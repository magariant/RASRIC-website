ABOUT

RASRIC is the independent studio of Oakland-based muralist, painter, and designer Tom Magarian. Specializing in vibrant, surreal imagery, RASRIC creates large-scale public artworks that fuse bold colors, whimsical characters, and imaginative storytelling. With a mission to revitalize underrepresented spaces, the work aims to spark dialogue, uplift communities, and bring moments of beauty into everyday environments. Grounded in a commitment to creative expression and social connection, RASRIC transforms walls into meaningful, engaging visual experiences.
